Weeks 1 & 2 meal plan

Full liquid diet. No chewing. Everything consumed through a squeeze bottle. The goal is 90g+ protein daily, 2.5L fluid, and two collagen doses to support bone and tissue healing.

Daily target
1,980 kcal
Protein
103 g
Fluid
2.5 L

Notes for the caregiver

✦ Collagen ingredients are marked in green throughout. Always pair with Vitamin C 500mg — it significantly enhances collagen absorption and bone healing. The first three weeks post-surgery are when the body’s demand for collagen is highest.
Do not take Percocet and Klonopin together under any circumstances. Follow Dr. Movahed’s Pain Management Schedule exactly and log all medication times.
