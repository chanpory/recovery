Chanpory’s double jaw surgery recovery guide

I’m having maxillomandibular advancement (MMA) surgery on April 9 to treat sleep apnea. Recovery is long and difficult. My energy and mobility will be very limited, I’ll be on a liquid-only diet for the first two weeks, and I won’t be able to chew for at least two months. This site is a guide for the family and friends who’ll be helping me through it. Thank you for being here. I couldn’t do this without you.
