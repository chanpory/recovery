Medications

Critical safety note
Do not take Percocet and Klonopin at the same time. Follow Dr. Movahed's pain management schedule exactly. Log every dose and the time it was given.

Pain management

Percocet(oxycodone/acetaminophen)

  • Take on schedule as directed — don't wait for pain to escalate before taking a dose.
  • Log every dose on the whiteboard: medication name, dose, and exact time given.
  • Never combine with Klonopin. The two must not be taken at the same time under any circumstances.

Muscle relaxer

Klonopin(clonazepam)

  • Minimum 1 month post-op. Some patients need longer — follow Dr. Movahed's guidance.
  • Must be properly tapered off. Never stop abruptly — this can cause serious withdrawal effects.
  • Do not take at the same time as Percocet.

Antibiotic

Augmentin(amoxicillin/clavulanic acid)

  • Fill the prescription immediately after hospital discharge — do not wait.
  • Complete the entire course. Do not stop early even if I'm feeling better.

Oral rinse

Peridex(chlorhexidine gluconate 0.12%)

  • Swish and spit 3 times daily for the first 2 weeks.
  • After every meal: rinse with warm salt water first, then follow with Peridex.
  • This routine is critical for preventing infection around the sutures.

Sinus care

Congestion is normal and can last for months after MMA surgery.

Navage sinus rinse

  • Start 1 week post-op.
  • Use in the shower — it's easier to manage and less mess.

Sudafed-D and Flonase

  • Take both twice daily for congestion.

Dairy

  • Avoid dairy for the first month — it increases mucus production and worsens congestion.

Supplements

Vitamin C 500mg

  • Take twice daily.
  • Always take alongside collagen — Vitamin C is required for collagen synthesis and significantly enhances absorption.

Multivitamins

  • Take twice daily.

Probiotics

  • Take as directed. Especially important while on Augmentin to maintain gut health.
